# Services

PulseBoard provides a compact service portfolio for leaderboard-driven gaming products.

## Core Services

- **Leaderboard Delivery**: server-side rendered pages and JSON APIs for ranking data.
- **Player Management**: create, update, and delete player records over API.
- **Insights Snapshot**: top players, average score, and announcements for business visibility.

## Integration Support

Teams can integrate PulseBoard data into internal tools by consuming `/api/leaderboard` and `/api/users`.
