# About PulseBoard

PulseBoard supports gaming organizations that need transparent and trustworthy ranking systems.
The service is built to serve high read traffic while preserving score integrity during updates.

## Mission

Deliver a leaderboard experience that is both engaging for players and useful for operators.

## Principles

1. Ranking accuracy above vanity metrics.
2. Clear and explainable player data.
3. Operational simplicity for product and support teams.
